Tony Stark / Iron Man (MCU)

The man who saved half the universe

Here's a timeline of Tony Stark's life:

  • 1970 - Born in Manhattan, NY, son to Howard and Maria Stark.
  • 1977 - Attended Phillips Academy in Andover, MA.
  • 1986 - Wins 4th Annual MIT Robot Design Award.
  • 1991 - Howard and Maria die assassinated.
  • 1992 - Takes over Obadiah Stane's role as CEO of Stark Industries.
  • 2009 - Travels to Afghanistan to showcase a new wepon and gets kidnapped by the Ten Rings.
  • 2009 - Builds the first iteration of his Iron Man armor.
  • 2012 - Joins the Avengers.
  • 2015 - Creates Ultron.
  • 2015 - Leaves the Avengers.
  • 2016 - Fights Captain America in the Superheroe's Civil War.
  • 2018 - Fights the Titan, Thanos resulting in the death of half the Universe's population.
  • 2023 - Figures out time travel through the Quantum Realm.
  • 2023 - Sacrifices himself to bring back half the universe.
